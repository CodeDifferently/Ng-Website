About us

Founded in July 2018, Code Differently is an experiential learning coding program designed to introduce and elevate information technology to youth through hands-on practical instruction. Code Differently was created by local entrepreneurs with a collective passion to bridge the gap between education and local market needs by equipping communities with the skills needed to excel in the 21st century.

Our Mission

It is our mission to create an ecosystem of technology focused resources through creative programming and partnerships aimed at building an inclusive pipeline of technology talent that is positioned for the current and future workforce needs.

Coding Differently

Community engagement is one of our core principles. Deploying our coding camps in “pop-up” locations allows us to engage with our students and their families in the communities where they live, work and play. With technology changing the way we do nearly every job, from manual labor to banking to healthcare to law enforcement,Code Differently exists to bolster a culture of learning that is vital now and will be essential in the future.

Strategic Pillars

Our coding programs are designed to support innovative employment opportunities by introducing and elevating technology through practical hands-on instruction for K-12, higher education and experienced professionals.

  • Attract participants via innovative initiatives that spark interest
  • Provide exposure to the amazing world of tech and coding
  • Promote engagement to foster a deeper learning of principles and concepts
  • Provide opportunities for participants to excel by developing real technology solutions
  • Empower participants to inspire their peers and younger counterparts to explore tech related courses and fields
