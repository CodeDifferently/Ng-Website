We know this community; this is our community. We are invested in this work; this is our work. Code Differently is not a venture by a capitalist looking to add to a portfolio; it is the natural continuation of the purpose and passion of two accomplished professionals in the tech field who call Delaware home. We are committed to reversing the statistics that say minorities don’t enter into tech fields or that kids are not interested in learning coding; our work and experience with Code Differently has already proven those assertions wrong and the limiting reactant is exposure. Our experience and numerous studies have shown that exposing kids to tech and coding increases their interest in the field; our life’s’ work is to ensure that when today’s generation enters the workforce, their peers standing right beside them ready to blaze the trails. We look forward to working with you!
